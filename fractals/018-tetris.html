Perfect Tetris: Infinite Gameplay

This is a self-playing Tetris game powered by an AI that makes optimal decisions for every piece. The AI evaluates potential placements based on multiple factors: holes created, height penalties, bumpiness of the surface, and complete lines. By looking ahead multiple pieces, it can plan sequences that maintain a flat, manageable playing field indefinitely.

The fractal nature of Tetris emerges from the recursive pattern-matching required for optimal play. Each piece placement creates a new state that must be evaluated recursively. The AI's decision tree branches exponentially with look-ahead depth, exploring thousands of possible futures to find the best path forward. This creates a self-similar decision-making process at every level of play.

Watch as the AI maintains perfect balance, clearing lines efficiently while keeping the board height low. The game theoretically never ends - the AI can play indefinitely by following optimal strategies. Adjust the speed to watch rapid-fire perfect play, or slow it down to observe the AI's decision-making process. Increase look-ahead depth for even more optimal play, though it becomes computationally intensive.
